About

Hi! I'm a Sophomore at Duke University studying Physics, Math, and Computer Science and am an incoming summer intern at the Duke Quantum Center. For the past two years, I've been on a gap year, and I've spent my time programming, taking courses on MIT OCW, trading stocks, and playing chess. I'm currently interested in human longevity, the intersection between theoretical computer science and physics, and anything that'll help me understand existence. Source for this website.
